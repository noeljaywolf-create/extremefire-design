What We Install

Systems We Design & Maintain

Automatic Sprinkler & Pre-action Systems

Heat-activated suppression that reacts in seconds to control fires at their source. Only the sprinklers above the fire activate — minimising water damage.

  • Wet, dry, and pre-action arrangements
  • Hydraulic calculations per NFPA 13 / EN 12845
  • Full installation coordination
Download Overview PDF

Fire Hydrant & Hose-Reel Systems

Reliable water supply infrastructure for firefighting — from underground mains feeding external hydrants to wall-mounted hose reels for first-response teams.

  • Internal and external hydrant networks
  • Hose-reel layouts and installations
  • Water supply adequacy review

Detection & Alarm Systems

Early warning systems that detect fire before it spreads — from smoke and heat sensors to addressable panels that pinpoint the exact device and location.

  • Conventional and addressable panels
  • Smoke, heat, and flame detection
  • Device layouts and programming
Download Overview PDF

Deluge & Water Spray Systems

High-volume water application for the most demanding hazards — open nozzles flood entire areas when triggered by detection systems.

  • Industrial and high-hazard protection
  • Hazard-based system design
  • Commissioning and testing support

Gas, Foam & Dry Chemical Suppression

Special hazard systems for environments where water isn't the answer — clean agents protect electronics, foam blankets flammable liquids, dry chemical covers industrial risks.

  • FM-200, Novec, inert gas systems
  • Foam suppression for fuel storage
  • Dry chemical for industrial hazards

Water Mist Systems

Fine water droplets that suppress fires with minimal water usage — ideal for sensitive equipment, power infrastructure, and enclosed industrial spaces.

  • Generators and power transformers
  • Switchgear buildings and cable tunnels
  • Turbine houses and boiler rooms

How We Work

From Assessment to Commissioning

01

Hazard Assessment
