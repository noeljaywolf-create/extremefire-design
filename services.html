What We Do

DESIGN · INSTALL · MAINTAIN

We deliver complete fire protection solutions across every project phase, tailored to your facility and supported by experienced engineers.

DESIGN

Complete AutoCAD layouts and hydraulic calculations

We prepare complete, accurate drawings for reviewers, stocklisters, installers, contractors, and project stakeholders. Our plans include fully dimensioned layouts, main and branch line identification, hanger positions, cut lengths, and hydraulic calculation support.

  • AutoCAD fire protection layouts
  • Computerized hydraulic calculations
  • Material schedules, pipe sizing, and installation details

INSTALLATION & COMMISSIONING

Experienced technicians for all fire system installations

Experienced technicians support fire detection, suppression, extinguishing, sprinkler, hydrant, hose-reel, and special hazard system installations. Workshop prefabrication can reduce site welding, threading, and assembly time.

  • System installation coordination
  • Testing and commissioning support
  • As-built drawings and project handover documentation

MAINTENANCE & RAPID RESPONSE

Inspection, testing, and ongoing maintenance programs

The service team supports alterations, extensions, annual servicing, fire and plumbing installations, and six-monthly servicing of sprinkler control valves. The goal is dependable response and systems that remain ready.

  • Inspection, testing, and maintenance programs
  • System alterations and extensions
  • Audits, pipe analysis, water supply review, and upgrade advice
