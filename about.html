About Extreme Fire

Extreme Fire Design Inc is a fire protection engineering, installation, and maintenance company serving industries that need dependable fire detection, suppression, extinguishing, sprinkler, hydrant, and special hazard protection.

The company focuses on innovative technology, superior engineering, quality workmanship, strong customer relationships, and practical systems that are tailored to each project.
